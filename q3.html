Guiding Points for Response

Curriculum Parameters

Parameters are the constraints and requirements that shape your design decisions.

  • Purpose: Why does this curriculum exist? (Skill gap vs Compliance vs New Product).
  • Subject Matter: Is it Knowledge (cognitive), Skills (psychomotor), or Attitude (affective)?
  • Learner Profile: Experience level, literacy, location.

Design Decisions Affected

  • Entry Requirements: Prerequisites needed.
  • Assessment Methods: Written test vs Practical performance.
  • Delivery Pathways/Methods: Online vs Classroom vs On-the-job.
  • Sequencing: How units are ordered.

Example: Parameter Influence

Parameter Analysis Influence on Decision
Learner Profile:
Gen Z agents, high mobile usage, short attention span.		 Delivery Method: Selected Microlearning videos delivered via mobile app for product updates to increase engagement.
