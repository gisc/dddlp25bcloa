Guiding Points for Response

Emerging Trends

Select ONE trend to discuss impact:

  • Changing Nature of Work: Remote/Hybrid work, Gig economy.
  • Learning for Performance: Just-in-time learning, performance support tools vs courses.
  • Digital Technology: AI, VR/AR, Adaptive learning platforms.
  • "Power Skills" (Soft Skills): Empathy, resilience, critical thinking.
  • Skills-First: Hiring/developing based on skills rather than degrees/titles.

Possible Responses

  • Adopt practice-based approaches.
  • Develop blended learning designs.
  • Focus on adaptability and "learning to learn".
