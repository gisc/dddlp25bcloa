Guiding Points for Response

Types of Stakeholder Input

Consulting stakeholders confirms objectives and parameters. Common inputs include:

  • Organisational Factors: Business goals, budget, timelines, KPIs.
  • Industrial/Government Requirements: Compliance standards, WSQ frameworks, safety regulations.
  • Learner Characteristics: Demographics, tech-savviness, language proficiency, motivation.
  • Competitor Practices: Market benchmarks (what are others doing?).

Impact on Design

  • How did the input shape the "look and feel"?
  • Did it constrain the budget or timeline?
  • Did it define the necessary content depth?

Example: Stakeholder Impact

Stakeholder Input Impact on Curriculum Design
Operations Manager (Org Factor)
"We cannot pull agents off the phones for 2 full days."		 Shift to Blended Learning: Redesigned the 2-day workshop into 4 hours of self-paced e-learning (theory) + 4 hours of intense face-to-face practice.
QA Team (Internal Requirement)
"Agents are failing the compliance greeting 30% of the time."		 Added Specific Objective: Included a mandatory "Compliance Greeting" drill and assessment criteria in the role-play rubric.

Contextualise to your scenario.
