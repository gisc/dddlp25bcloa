DQ8: Describe at least 2 potential challenges faced during curriculum and instruction implementation and suggest mitigating solutions.

Guiding Points for Response

Common Obstacles (The 3 Ps)

  • People: Communication barriers, team conflicts, and lack of stakeholder buy-in.
  • Power: Organizational politics, unrealistic expectations, and shifting priorities (internal or external factors).
  • Process: Unclear project planning and underestimation of required time, resources, or expertise.

Mitigation Strategies

  • Change Management: Structured approach to manage transition and resistance.
  • Communication & Negotiation: Clear channels and conflict resolution skills to align stakeholders.
  • Project Management: Robust planning, resource tracking, and timeline management.
  • Evaluation Framework: Use data to validate results and guide improvements.

Example: Validation Findings & Recommendations

Findings (Challenges) Recommendations (Solutions)
Entry Requirements too High
Stakeholders agreed that requiring participants to be "Degree Holders" was too restrictive for the target audience. 		Adjust Requirement
Re-worded to "Possess strong analytical skills with at least a diploma" to broaden accessibility while maintaining standards.
Assessment Resource Intensity
HR Director flagged that live role-plays were too time-consuming and resource-intensive for the assessors. 		Streamline Assessment
Replaced live role-play with a video submission of the learner's best in-class performance, significantly reducing assessment meeting time.
